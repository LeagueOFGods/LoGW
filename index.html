    Hello & Welcome

    The latest updates on LoG will be posted on this on this website. We strive to keep our members happy while we maintain a professional standard of work. If you are a member of LoG, you have the option receive pay 4 times daily! ​

    • Daily X4 Pay - 6AM, 6PM, 12 AM, 12PM GMT
    • Daily events and weekly competitions to keep our members satisfied
    • Amazing Discord server perks as a member of League of Gods

    Here at the League of Gods, we strive to be the best. We have a strong focus on ensuring that our community is a good community. When you join LoG, you become a part of the family. We are a UNIQUE Habbo agency and we aim to make Habbo a better place for everyone!

    Our agency is different from many others. Founded in November 2020 by Elacys & Headache, LoG has an original concept and theme which is based upon. We base our ranks, base and everything LoG related on Greek Mythology and the Gods!



    Transfer Unit

    This unit handles the incoming transfers of people wanting to join the League of Gods from other agencies across Habbo. They also handle the control of the Conversion Chart.



    Roman Legionary

    This unit handles all Internal Affairs that the League of Gods may face or need. They handle disputes that occur within the base & handle Worker of the Week / Month awards.



    Roman Diplomacy

    This unit handles all External Affairs that the League of Gods may face or need. They handle relationships with other agencies & monitor the National Punishment lists.



    Olympian Games

    This unit handles all of the events and games that occur within the League of Gods. They ensure that events are happening often to keep the morale high within LoG!



    Olympian Media

    This unit handles all of the Media & Newspaper as well as social media accounts of LoG!



    Trial Aesymnetes

    Trial Aesymnetes iC Cabinet help to train our fresh new iC candidates, to make sure that they are fit and ready to fulfill their new responsibilities.
